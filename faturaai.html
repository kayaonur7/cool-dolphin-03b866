Features

Single invoice, bulk analysis, full control.

🤖

Auto-reading with AI

Powered by Claude Sonnet — automatically extracts company name, invoice number, VAT, total, and all line items from invoice images.

📄

PDF & e-Archive Support

Supports photos, PDFs, and e-Archive HTML invoices. Multi-page analysis included.

📦

Bulk Import

Select dozens of invoices at once. The app analyzes them sequentially and reports success/error status.

📊

Excel Export

Export all your invoices to Excel with one tap. Ready to send to your accountant or archive.

🔁

Duplicate Protection

Prevents creating duplicate records with the same invoice number. Eliminates accidental double-entry.

🔒

Privacy First

Data is stored on your device. Invoice images are sent to Anthropic's API for analysis only and not retained anywhere else.

How it works

📷

1. Upload invoice

Take a photo, select a PDF, or open an e-Archive HTML file.

🧠

2. AI analyzes

Claude Sonnet reads the invoice and extracts structured data in seconds.

✅

3. Confirm & save

Review extracted information, edit if needed, and save.

📤

4. Export

Export all your invoices to Excel. Ready format for accounting.
